Aegis Initiative (Early Access)

Aegis Initiative is an on rail flyer/shooter that takes players to various exotic planets and whose game play varies based on your copilots and their unique abilities. Grow with your fellow pilots through unique dialogue in the visual novel sections

Systems Officer

Manage the GitHub repository and decide when changes are good in order to be merged into the main branch as well as do small updates to systems, organizing the project, and fix issues.

Copilots System

Created the base for copilots system to facilitate and standardize the method of making copilots and adding their unique abilities to the player.

UI functionality

Created the scripts to handle all of the UI functionality in the game. This included things like settings, loading scenes, and Copilot selection.

Asteroids

Script that spawned asteroids within an area and kept them there as debri for the player to try and evade.

Enemy Grunt

Created the scripts for an enemy grunt asset that plays an animation and spawn bullets that damage the player.
