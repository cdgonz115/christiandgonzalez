Full Project Commit History

The Full commit History to showcase in detail all of my contributions to this project

Player Movement

Created a movement system that allows the player to build speed as long as they hold down one of the move keys or they crash with a wall.

Teleporter

Made a teleporter that matches the players postion to allow them to build speed and position themselves to reach new spots with their increased speed.

Break Wall

Made the script that destroys the walls if they are over teh required velocity to break them.
